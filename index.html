Welcome to the Accessibility Hub

A place for the Infinum NA team to see resources and best practices gathered together.

What is this?

The Accessibility Hub is a shared reference for everyone involved in building digital products — business development, design, production, and engineering. It was created to bring resources and guidelines from across disciplines and sources to one location.

Accessibility (often abbreviated as a11y) is the practice of ensuring that digital products can be used by everyone, including people with disabilities.

WCAG at a glance

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are the internationally recognised standard for accessible web content. The current version is WCAG 2.2, with three conformance levels:

  • Level A – Minimum requirements
  • Level AA – The target required by most legislation
  • Level AAA – The highest level of accessibility

All guidance in this hub targets WCAG 2.2 Level AA as a baseline.
